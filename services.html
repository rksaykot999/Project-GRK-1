Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and pricing.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A typical website takes 4-6 weeks, web applications 8-12 weeks, and enterprise solutions 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the discovery phase and use agile methodologies to ensure timely delivery.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes, all our projects include post-launch support. Our support packages range from 3 months for basic projects to 12 months for enterprise solutions. We also offer ongoing maintenance plans to ensure your digital products remain secure, updated, and performing optimally.

Can you work with our existing team?

Absolutely. We frequently collaborate with in-house teams, providing expertise where needed. Our flexible engagement models allow us to augment your existing team, provide technical leadership, or handle entire projects based on your requirements.

What technologies do you specialize in?

We work with a wide range of modern technologies including React, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, Flutter, React Native, AWS, Azure, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, and more. We choose the best technology stack for each project based on requirements, scalability needs, and long-term maintainability.

How do you ensure project quality?

We follow industry best practices including code reviews, automated testing, continuous integration, and regular client reviews. Our quality assurance process includes functional testing, performance testing, security testing, and user acceptance testing to ensure the highest quality deliverables.
